Anisotropic unstructured mesh generation for complex geometries

Université Paris-Est Créteil (UPEC)
Créteil, France
October 2022 - October 2023
The course introduces the theoretical background and methods for anisotropic unstructured mesh generation.
  1. Simplexes and barycentric coordinates
  2. Linked lists and hash tables
  3. Delaunay kernel
  4. Riemannian manifold and metrics
  5. Anisotropic meshes
  6. Mesh adaptation

Incompressible fluid mechanics [MF102]

ENSTA Paris
Palaiseau, France
June 2019
The goal of this course is to lay out the fundamental concepts and results for incompressible fluid mechanics.
  1. Characteristic quantities of an incompressible flow
  2. Dimensional analysis
  3. Navier-Stokes equations
  4. Boundary layer concepts
  5. Matched asymptotic expansions
  6. Falkner-Skan boundary layer

Fundamentals to understand and analyze high fidelity compressible Large Eddy Simulation (LES)

CERFACS
Toulouse, France
May 2016
This training course enables the participants to reinforce their theoretical knowledge in order to understand and analyze high fidelity compressible Large Eddy Simulation (LES) and hands-on sessions are organized to put the theoretical courses into practice.
  1. Numerical simulation for the Navier-Stokes equations (mesh, discretization methods, data storage)
  2. High-fidelity numerical schemes for LES
  3. Signal processing and unsteady turbulence modeling
  4. Turbulence injection methods
  5. Navier-Stokes Characteristic Boundary Conditions (NSCBC)
  6. Wall treatment
